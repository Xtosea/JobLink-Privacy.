JobLink Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information: Name, email, phone number, and other details you provide in your job application.
  • Uploaded documents: CVs, proofs of payment, or other files submitted.
  • Usage information: Device info, app usage data, and interactions with our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To review and improve submitted CVs.
  • To apply for suitable jobs on your behalf.
  • To train and prepare you for interviews.
  • To communicate updates or instructions via email or WhatsApp/Telegram.
  • To improve our services and app functionality.

3. Sharing of Information

Your information is only accessible to authorized JobLink admins. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your information securely in our backend systems. We take reasonable steps to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at: joblinkhelpdesk@gmail.com

6. Third-Party Services

JobLink may use third-party services (e.g., cloud storage, analytics) which have their own privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected with an updated effective date.
